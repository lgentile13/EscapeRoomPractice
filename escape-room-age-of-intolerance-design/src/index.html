Description

My fellow trustee....you have been selected to travel back to the Roaring 20's, a time of partying, fun, and Intolerance. There is a mission I need you to complete for me. Mr. and Mrs. Albrecht have donated millions to Long Island and its reformations and they are highly valued. Someone has recently stolen and hidden Mrs. Clara Albrecht's ten million dollar daimond necklace from her home. This necklace is a family heirloom and it's absence has made Mrs. Albrecht tremendously distressed. We are aware that the necklace has been hidden somewhere in the 80 acre Warren Bamford Mansion. Tonight there will be party at this mansion which is located at the tip of Long Island near the Montauk Lighthouse. I will need you to attend this gathering and stay under the radar. Mr. and Mrs. Albrecht will take part in this festivity along side vast amounts of security. I will need you to find clues to where this necklace could be. Think you could help me find it without getting caught?

The time is 1920... It's around 7:00 P.M. on August 10. The sun is resting on the water slowly going down as the moon awakens. You hear the car doors shutting as people are arriving, the loud chatter and laughter of people mingling, and the alluring music that gets louder and louder as you enter the lively party.

Cipher Message

This puzzle will be a letter that you will receive at your table. It will be difficult to undertsand unless you can decode it. The decoded version of the message will lead you to your next location where a clue will be provided.

Recorded Audio

This puzzle will be a recording you will need to listen to and listen carefully. A clue will be given, subtly, to help you get your next location. Be sure to take notes, it will help later on.

Picture Puzzle

This puzzle will require you to piece the pictures together to make one big picture. The pictures will be broken up into smaller pictures. There will be three of these pictures. The whole pictures will provide you with your next clue.

Guess the Number

This puzzle will require you to guess a number 1-100. If you guess the right number you will be rewarded. The reward is a clue for the next puzzle.

Matching Game

This will require you to match images together. There will be two of each image. A hint to your next clue will then be revealed to you once you match all pictures correctly.

Extraction

This puzzle will provide you with a reading passage. This passage will secretly give you information. You should right down what you think is important. You will need the informatio to figure out your next clue.

Conclusion

Congratulations friend, you did it! You have safely found Mrs. Albrecht's daimond necklace and without getting caught! She appreciates all you have done and has decided to reward you with a free Escape Room Game. We will send you back home tonight. We hope to see you again soon and thank you again for your great work.
